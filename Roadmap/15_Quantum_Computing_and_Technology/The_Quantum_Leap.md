## **1. What is Quantum Computing?**
Classical computers use **Bits** (0 or 1). Quantum computers use **Qubits** (0, 1, or both at the same time). 
- Imagine a coin spinning on a table. While it's spinning, it is neither heads nor tails. It is a **Superposition**.
- This allows a quantum computer to explore millions of solutions simultaneously.

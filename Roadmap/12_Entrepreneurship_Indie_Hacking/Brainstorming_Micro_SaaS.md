# **Phase 12: Entrepreneurship - Brainstorming Micro-SaaS**

A "Micro-SaaS" is a small software-as-a-service that solves a very specific problem for a specific niche. 

---

## **1. The Brainstorming Framework**
**[Niche] + [Painful Manual Task] + [Automation/Simplification] = 💰**

### **Example 1: The Content Creator**
- **Niche:** YouTube Creators.
- **Pain:** Manually creating "Chapters" and "Timestamps" for 1-hour videos.
- **Solution:** A MERN app that uses AI to auto-generate timestamps from the video transcript.

### **Example 2: The Real Estate Agent**
- **Niche:** Local Real Estate Agents.
- **Pain:** Following up with 100 leads every day via WhatsApp.
- **Solution:** A simple CRM that sends automated, personalized follow-ups.

### **Example 3: The E-commerce Owner**
- **Niche:** Shopify Store Owners.
- **Pain:** Tracking if their ads are actually profitable across 3 different platforms.
- **Solution:** A dashboard that pulls data from Meta, Google, and Shopify to show "Net Profit" in real-time.

---

## **2. Why Micro-SaaS?**
- **Low Competition:** Big companies don't care about small niches.
- **High Loyalty:** Once a user finds a tool that solves their specific niche problem, they never leave.
- **Solo-Friendly:** One developer can build and maintain it.

# **Phase 12: Entrepreneurship - Realistic Timeline (0 to $1k MRR)**

Building a startup is a marathon, not a sprint. Here is a realistic 12-month roadmap for a solo founder.

---

## **Month 1-2: The Learning & Hunting Phase**
- **Goal:** Master the MERN stack (Phase 6) and find a problem.
- **Action:** Talk to potential users (on Reddit, Twitter, or local businesses).
- **Milestone:** A validated idea and a rough sketch of your MVP.

---

## **Month 3-4: The Building Phase**
- **Goal:** Build the Minimum Viable Product (MVP).
- **Action:** Build ONLY the core feature. No "About Us" page, no fancy settings. Just the solution.
- **Milestone:** A working app that a user can log into and use.

---

## **Month 5: The "Soft Launch"**
- **Goal:** Get your first 10 users (even if free).
- **Action:** Share it on "Show HN" (Hacker News), Product Hunt, and niche subreddits.
- **Milestone:** Your first real user feedback.

---

## **Month 6-8: Iteration & First Dollar**
- **Goal:** Get your first paying customer.
- **Action:** Fix the bugs found in Month 5. Add a "Pricing" page.
- **Milestone:** **$1 to $100 MRR.** This is the hardest part.

---

## **Month 9-12: Marketing & Growth**
- **Goal:** Scale to $1,000 MRR.
- **Action:** Focus 80% on marketing and 20% on code. Use SEO, content marketing, or cold emails.
- **Milestone:** **$1,000 MRR.** At this point, your startup is likely paying for your basic lifestyle.

---

## **⚖️ The Reality Check**
- **90% of startups fail.** The secret is to "fail fast" and "fail cheap."
- **Burnout is real.** Don't work 100 hours a week. Work 20 high-quality hours.
- **Cashflow is King.** If you have a job or savings, use them to extend your timeline.

---

## **🎯 Exercise for You:**
Set a "Launch Date" for your MVP exactly 60 days from today. Mark it in your calendar. Everything you do from now on must lead to that date.

## **2. Classical Machine Learning**

Before deep learning, you must master the basics:
- **Supervised Learning:**
    - **Regression:** Predicting a value (e.g., house prices).
    - **Classification:** Predicting a category (e.g., Spam vs Not Spam).
- **Unsupervised Learning:**
    - **Clustering:** Grouping similar items (e.g., customer segments).
    - **Dimensionality Reduction:** Simplifying data (PCA).

---

## **3. The Machine Learning Workflow**

1. **Data Collection:** Getting the raw data.
2. **Preprocessing:** Handling missing values and scaling.
3. **Model Selection:** Choosing the right algorithm.
4. **Training:** Letting the computer learn.
5. **Evaluation:** Checking Accuracy, Precision, and Recall.

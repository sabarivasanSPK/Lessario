# **Phase 5: Aptitude & Logic (Round 1)**

Round 1 is the filter. Even the best coders can fail if they underestimate Aptitude.

---

## **1. Quantitative Aptitude (The Math)**

- **Time, Speed & Distance:** Problems on trains, boats, and streams.
- **Time & Work:** "If A does a job in 5 days and B in 10 days..."
- **Percentages, Profit & Loss:** The basics of business math.
- **Permutations & Combinations / Probability:** Very common for higher-level logic.

---

## **2. Logical Reasoning**

- **Blood Relations:** "Pointing to a man, Ravi said..."
- **Seating Arrangements:** Linear and Circular.
- **Syllogisms:** "All cats are dogs. Some dogs are tigers..."
- **Coding-Decoding:** "If ZOHO is coded as 1234..."

---

## **3. Output Prediction (C/Java)**

A significant portion of Round 1 involves predicting the output of tricky code snippets.
- **Focus on:**
    - Static blocks and variables.
    - Post/Pre-increment (`i++` vs `++i`).
    - Bitwise operations.
    - Operator precedence.

---

## **4. Resources**
- **Indiabix:** Great for basic practice.
- **GeeksforGeeks Aptitude:** For higher-level logical questions.

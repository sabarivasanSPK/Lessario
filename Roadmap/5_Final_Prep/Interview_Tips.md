# **Phase 5: The Final Interviews (Technical & HR)**

You've solved the problems, you've built the system. Now you need to sell yourself.

---

## **1. The Technical Discussion**

In Round 5, you will talk about your code from Round 4.
- **Be ready to explain:**
    - "Why did you use an `ArrayList` instead of a `LinkedList`?"
    - "How would you add a 'Waiting List' feature if the requirement changed?"
    - "What is the time complexity of your booking logic?"

---

## **2. Behavioral (HR) Questions**

Zoho looks for **Humility** and **Willingness to Learn**.

- **"Tell me about yourself":** Focus on your coding journey.
- **"Why Zoho?":** Mention their focus on craftsmanship and long-term product vision.
- **"How do you handle failure?":** Give a real example of a bug you couldn't solve and how you eventually did.

---

## **3. The Zoho "Culture Fit"**

- **Humble Beginnings:** They love stories of people who learned from scratch (like you!).
- **Problem Solvers:** They prefer someone who can solve a problem with basic logic over someone who knows 10 frameworks but can't print a pattern.
- **Stability:** They value people who want to stay and grow with the company.

---

## **4. Final Checklist**
- [ ] Resume is clean and highlights logic/problem-solving.
- [ ] You have 1-2 non-Zoho projects (even if simple).
- [ ] You are comfortable explaining code on a whiteboard/paper.
- [ ] Your attire is professional (Business casual is fine for Zoho).

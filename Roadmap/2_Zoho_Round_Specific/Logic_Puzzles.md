# **Phase 2: Logic & Mathematical Puzzles**

Round 2 and Round 3 at Zoho often feature problems that aren't just about arrays, but about **Mathematical Logic**.

---

## **1. Number-Based Logic**

### **A. Weight of Numbers**
**Problem:** Given a set of numbers, calculate their weight based on the following:
- 5 points if it's a perfect square.
- 4 points if it's a multiple of 4 and divisible by 6.
- 3 points if it's an even number.
**Task:** Sort the numbers based on their weights.

### **B. Base Conversions**
Converting Decimal to Binary/Hexadecimal or custom bases (e.g., "Excel Column Title" logic).

---

## **2. Array/String Logic Puzzles**

### **A. Look and Say Sequence**
1, 11, 21, 1211, 111221...
(The next term is the "count" and "digit" of the previous term).

### **B. String Decoding**
**Problem:** Given "a2b3c1", output "aabbbc".
**Advanced:** Given "a(b2)3", output "abbabbabb".

---

## **3. Bitwise Magic**

Zoho occasionally asks about bitwise operations for optimization.
- `n & 1 == 0` (Check if even)
- `n >> 1` (Divide by 2)
- `n << 1` (Multiply by 2)

---

## **🎯 Exercise for You:**
1. **Find the Odd Occurring Element:** Given an array where every element appears twice except for one, find that one element. (Hint: Use XOR operator `^`).
2. **Matrix Rotation:** Rotate a square matrix 90 degrees clockwise **in-place**.

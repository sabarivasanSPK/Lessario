Singu - The Raja
Episode 7 - The Unstoppable Force
Runtime: ~22 minutes
Written by - Santhosh Mathevan

## SCENE 1 – EXT – DAY – A FOREST PATH BY RIVER

A narrow elevated path between rocky formations by the river.
Muttagose carefully balancing.

**MUTTAGOSE**
“Idhu konjam thrill ah irukku… aana semma fun ah-vum irukku!”
Suttapazham is walking slowly.
Suddenly, they hear someone behind the bushes.
Muttagose panics listening to that, slips and runs in the same spot
and finally falls.

**SUTTAPAZHAM**
“Sssaarapaambu… Neeyaa adhu?”

**SSSAARAPAAMBU**
“Naan inga irukken”
He appears out of nowhere from Suttapazham’s hump.
He gets scared.

**SUTTAPAZHAM**
“Aathaadi… Veshaththa koththi giththi vechuraatha…”
Sssaarapaambu giggles.
They hear the same sound behind the bushes again.
When they all turn around.
A blur rushes past.
Dust everywhere.
Muttagose falls again.

**MUTTAGOSE**
“Ayyooo! Enna adhu?!”

## SCENE 2 – EXT – DAY – DAMAGED PATH

Animals gather.
The path is broken in parts.
Loose stones.
Cracks.

**MUTTAGOSE**
“Yethum Kaathu Karuppaa Irukkumo?”

**KONGINI**
“Kaathum kidayaathu karuppum kidaiyaathu… Yaarao romba speed ah
poraanga…”

**MUTTAGOSE**
“Athu yaaru ennavida speed ah… Intha kaattulayae naan thaan speed
aana mirugam…”
Singu clears his throat.

**MUTTAGOSE**
“Aadhaavthu… Naanthaan Rajavukku aduthu rendaavthu speed aana
mirugam”
Singu smiles.

**KONGINI**
“Muyalavida speed aana mirugam neriya iruku Muttagose… Kudhirai,
Vengai, Maan”

**MUTTAGOSE**
“Aama… Vengai, Sura, Singam, adhaan namma Raja… Animals perukku
badhilaa cinema padaththoda peraa sollitu irukeenga akka…”
And he giggles at his own jokes.
At that time.
Another distant impact sound travels across the forest.
Singu gets a hunch.
Singu jumps onto a rock.

**SINGU**
“My dear mirugangale, paravaigale, poochigale… Namma kaattukkulla
namakku pazhakkam illaadha oruthar vanthirukkaanga… Avanga yaarunu
kandupidikka vendiya neram vanthuruchu…”
The gang walks towards the sound.

## SCENE 3 – EXT - DAY - MONKEY COLONY

The monkeys' houses and tree branches are broken.
They are quarrelling as the gang enters their colony.
From far—
A powerful tiger silhouette.
Moving fast.
Disappears.

**MEENUKUTTI**
“Raja… anga yaaro asaiyura maathiri irunthuchu”

**MUTTAGOSE:**
“Raja… Avangala vidaathinga… Neenga ongi adicha ondra ton weight-uh
nu avangalukku kaattunga…”
Singu runs towards that direction.
He is shocked to see.
Multiple areas affected:
●  rocks falling
●  paths breaking
●  animals losing their homes.
Muttagose tries crossing a broken mud path.
Mud slides and Kongini catches her.
Suttapazham tries helping Sssaarapaambu.
Almost falls.
And the gang walks further to the small pond and see a few foot
prints of something similar to that of Singu.

## SCENE 4 – EXT - DAY - POND

Singu observes patterns.

**MUTTAGOSE**
“Yaaro namma kaatta thaakka varaanga pola…”

**SINGU**
“Idhu attack illa… idhu control illaadha movement.”
Muttagose looks at a few footprints and in one of the footprints of
that Tiger, Singu’s foot exactly matches.

**MUTTAGOSE**
“Raja… Engala prank panna try panringala… Neengathana adhu…”

**KONGINI**
“Singam mattum illama vera sila poonai vagai mirugangalukkum ithe
maathiri kaal thadam irukkum Muttagose…”
They all hear a roar from a cave behind.
Thambi appears.
Walks calmly now.
Everyone freezes.

**SINGU**
“Yaar nee… Unakku enna venum”
Singu roars and gets ready to pounce.

**THAMBI**
“Naan payanikka enakku oru vazhi venum… Adha naan eduththukkuren…”
He moves forward.
Pushes through the mud path.
Path breaks more.

**SINGU**
“Nee ippadi pona… kaadu moththamum azhinjirum…”

**THAMBI**
“Vegamaa odunaa thaan ennaala uyir vaazha mudiyum.”
The gang slowly moves towards the gap in the rocky path.
Muttagose tries jumping.
Fails.
Suttapazham tries bridging.
Fails.
Thambi jumps across easily.
Singu observes tree positions.
Creates a step sequence.
Leads everyone across safely.
They try to chase Thambi, but he swifts into the falling rocky zone.

## SCENE 5 – EXT - DAY - FALLING ROCK ZONE

Thambi’s movement loosened rocks above.
Rocks falling.
Kongini tries calculating.
Too slow.
Muttagose panics.
Tries running on a rolling rock, tries to balance, but loses it and
gets run over by the rock itself.
Sssaarapaambu laughs out loud at Muttgose.
But a rock falls on Sssaarapaambu, and he almost gets stuck.
Too many rocks start to follow.
Everyone starts to panic.
Singu goes to meditate.
Kumi…
Tisa…
Nane…
Saba…
Sita…
Tano…
Nne…
Tatu…
Mbili…
Moja…
He’s Super Singu now.
Singu watches rock pattern.
Breaks a whole tree and uses that to block the rocks.
Leads team through safely.
Thambi looks at Singu’s strength and mesmerizes.

## SCENE 6 – EXT - DAY - UNSTABLE SLOPE

Loose gravel slope.
Thambi runs straight.
Slides but recovers.
Singu follows but with slow and calculated steps.
Singu finally realises:
Problem is not terrain but an uncontrolled force
Singu finally blocks Thambi.

**SINGU**
“Ivalo balam irundhaa adha kattuppaduththa muyarchi pannu… Yeppo nee
un balaththa un control la vechurukiyo apo intha ulagaththula yetha
venumnaalum un control la veikkalaam”
Thambi tries pushing through.
Singu stands firm.

**SINGU**
“Ipo enna gavanichu paaru”
Then Singu demonstrates. Controlled movement. Calculated steps.
Thambi watches.
Tries again. This time slower. Controlled.
Terrain remains stable. Paths stabilise.

## SCENE 7 – EXT - DAY - RIVER BANK PATH

Animals move safely again.
Thambi looks at Singu.

**THAMBI**
“Vegam mattum thaan ennaoda balam nu nenachen… Aana unamiayaana
balamna ennanu puriya vechuteenga Raja”
Singu nods.
Muttgose looks at Thambi’s face and stripes on his body.

**MUTTAGOSE**
“Anna… Neenga Singu Rajavukku shave panni tattoo pota maathiri
irukinga… Unga peru enna?”

**THAMBI**
“Peraa… Enakku yaarum innum peru vaikkala.”

**SINGU**
“Peru illana enna. Nee inime enakku thambi maathiri.”
THAMBI murmurs “Thambi… Thambi…”

**THAMBI**
“En peru Thambi… Naan oru Puli…”
Every animal cheers.
Except Thanthiraa, who is upset.
THANTHIRAA (Talks to himself)
“Singuvae easy aana ethiri illa… Ipo Singuvukku oru thambi verayaa…
Paathukalam…”

**FINAL GAG SCENE – EXT - DAY - RIVER BANK PATH**
Muttagose tries “fast run” like Thambi.
Places his legs on a banana peel littered by a Monkey
Trips immediately.

**MUTTAGOSE**
“Fast vendam… Nammakku slow dhaan better!”
Everyone laughs.
